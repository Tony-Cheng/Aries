Aries

Description

In recent years, toxic comments and messages have become a major problem for online social platforms. Studies have shown that people sending and receiving toxic messages have a notable chance of developing depression and other social and mental disorders. Aries is an online web-based chat application tackling the problem of toxic messages by raising awareness of users' own toxic behaviour. By using neural networks, Aries identifies users' toxic messages and notify them of their toxic behaviour. Moreover, Aries compute a score between 0 and 1 for users to gauge their behaviour. Then, Aries has a recommendation system that suggests friends to users with a similar toxicity score.
